About us

Jesus Children Missions Outreach was founded by Mrs. Rosemary Odigbo in 2001 in the city of Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. Prior to starting the ministry, Mrs. Odigbo worked as a missionary for about a decade in Kwara State and other parts of Nigeria.

In many rural communities in Nigeria, poverty and less than Ideal socio-economic situations put the lives of many children at risk. Inadequate educational facilities, lack of proper nutrition and medical care centres, in general make many parts of rural Nigeria very high risk places to raise children. In view of this, Jesus Children Missions' focus is on less privileged children particularly from rural areas. Some of the children from these areas, especially the orphans who are generally socially and econonmically vulnerable, often lack proper care,physical security from abuse and molestation, education, adequate nutrition and proper clothing and medical care among other things. Girls raised in these environments are often forced into early marriages which greatly diminish their potential in life. Want to know more? You can check out our frequently asked question

Our vision

Our expectation in Jesus Children Missions Outreach is to put in place measures to improve the visibility of the children under our umbrella by providing homes, shelter facilities (Mission House) and schools for over 1000 identified children, in the next ten years and to empower and assist widows/widowers and sponsor missionaries in various ways as the need may arise, which include medical helps and to work closely with some people groups in established Mission bases.

  • Our goal at Jesus Children Missions Outreach is to provide shelter facilities (Mission House) and schools for over 1000 identified children, in the next ten years.
  • Produce future leaders equipped with innovative sound teaching, wisdom of God,integrity, sincerity, hard work, intelligence and godliness
  • The broad mission is that these children would become shining lights that would positively inflame their communities and the world at large
  • Train them to live a safe and productive life and play a meaningful role in the nation by the grace of God. Activities engaged in, are important step towards achieving brilliant future for the children in Nigeria and other countries, through the shelters, home base care with partners, and their families.
  • Help targeted children discover their God given potentials, and give them opportunity to excel in life.
  • The broad and long term vision is to produce a society that is healthy, safe and free from all forms of unhealthy vices.
