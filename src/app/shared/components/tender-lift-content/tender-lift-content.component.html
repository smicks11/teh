THE SCHOOL

Tenderlift Care Missions Schools which comprises of Nursery, Primary and Junior High Schools, in both urban and rural settings, suffered a setback just like other schools were affected by the lockdown. Though our major challenges still remain Staff/Teachers and the registration of the schools with the appropriate agencies in the State. Our motto is Sound Teaching and Wisdom. The challenges of funds for adequate running is still a limitation.

The population of the schools, in 2020 was 159, of which 70% were from the shelters. (righteousness, Peace and Joy Homes) 20% are orphaned and vulnerable in the community on scholarship while others are fee payers whose funds boost the financial obligations of the schools a little. Our major source of funding remain donations/charity.
